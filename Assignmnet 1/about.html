About Us

Established in 2023, The Solanki Sports Academy is a dynamic sports venture committed to promoting a vibrant sports culture nationwide. We offer comprehensive training and guidance across various sports such as Cricket, Football, Shooting, Yoga, Athletics, Self Defense, Physical fitness and Dance

Drawing from our extensive experience, we excel in organizing sports events, talent hunts, corporate tournaments, academy tournaments, and sports tours, solidifying our position in the sports community.

Our unique approach blends focused training, personalized attention, and constant motivation, enabling our students to attain honors on diverse platforms.
